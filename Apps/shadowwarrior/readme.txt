Shadow Warrior
--------------

Controls:

D-Pad: Move / Turn
Cross:  Crouch (Double press to toggle run mode)
Circle: Open / Activate
Triangle: Jump
Square: Fire 
L1: Next inventory item
L2: Strafe Left
R1: Next weapon
R2: Strafe Right
Select: Use inventory item
Start: Menu (Double press for map)


Note
----

The full version of Shadow Warrior Complete is available for free on 
GOG.com or on Steam - copy sw.grp and the MUSIC folder to 
/retroarch/apps/shadowwarrior to play on the PSC.
